About Dan

12 years teaching math in international schools across four countries.

Hi! I'm Dan Straffon. I've seen too many students fall through the cracks or get bored in math because they weren't getting the support or challenge they needed. I built this program as a place for students to get targeted instruction and challenge at a level that's appropriate for them. I believe that with the right conditions for growth, like small ability-based classes, targeted curriculum and consistent feedback, students can find great success and enjoyment in doing mathematics.

Our Math Tracks

Five tracks designed to meet students exactly where they are.

Core Tracks

Build

Grade 6 topics · RIT 215–229

Ratios, fractions, foundational problem-solving. This is where strong math thinking starts. We build the skills everything else depends on, and we make sure they're solid before moving on.

Momentum

Grade 7 topics · RIT 230–244

Proportional relationships, expressions, equations. Students here are ready to go deeper. We push past procedures and into real understanding, so they're not just getting answers but knowing why.

Ascend

Grade 8–9 topics · RIT 245–259

Linear relationships, functions, algebraic thinking. For students ready to move into high school level math. We make sure they don't just get there, but get there prepared.

Also Available

Foundations

Building core numeracy

Whole number operations, place value, fractions, decimals. For students who need to go back and fill in some gaps before moving forward. We rebuild core numeracy and number sense from the ground up, so the math ahead of them actually makes sense.

Problem Solving

Competition-style challenge

For kids who love math and want to be challenged — like, actually challenged. We're talking non-routine problems, multiple approaches, no hand-holding. Think Art of Problem Solving meets Math Olympiad.
