About Me

International school educator and founder of Straffon Math Academy.

Hi! Nice to meet you! My name is Dan Straffon and I've been teaching internationally since 2011. I was fortunate early in my career to be trained by a brilliant educator and was certified as a Math Specialist of International Schools. Since then, I've taught middle school math at top schools in Croatia, Argentina, China, and Peru. Most recently, I was the grade 7 and 8 math teacher at Colegio Roosevelt in Lima. I now teach online from Buenos Aires, Argentina.

I've spent most of my life overseas as a child of international school teachers. I'm a third culture kid through and through, which is why I continue to live and work abroad. These days I live in Buenos Aires with my wife and two dogs. I watch way too much soccer. I love a good asado. And I love teaching math, which I know sounds strange, but it's true.

Thanks for visiting. I built this program because I genuinely believe a small, ability-aligned class can be more powerful than a full classroom. Too often, kids are packed into classes with a huge range of levels and they just don't get the attention they deserve. They're capable of real math success. They just need someone who can actually see them. That's what this is for.
